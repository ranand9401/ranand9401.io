About Us

Mission & Vision

We are transforming the landscape of student education financing by implementing Community Pledge NetworksTM (CPN) at U.S. higher education institutions. A CPNTM is a pay-it-forward model that crowd sources alumni to directly sponsor student tuition. Upon graduation, students who benefited from the program are encouraged to pledge their support to the next cohort of students, thereby creating a cyclical, community based evergreening fund. This concept was initially developed at the University of Pittsburgh by Chancellor Patrick Gallagher, where it is currently being implemented. Our vision is to empower universities to better address the financial needs of their students while promoting alumni engagement through the revolutionary CPNTM model.

Company History

Contracted by the University of Pittsburgh in 2018, the team collaborated with the University of Pittsburgh administration to develop the CPNTM model.
